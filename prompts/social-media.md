## Usage Tips

### Hashtag Strategy by Platform

| Platform | Hashtag Count | Strategy |
|----------|--------------|----------|
| Instagram | 5-10 | Mix of niche (#AIcontentcreation) and broad (#productivity) |
| TikTok | 3-5 | Trending + niche. Check Discover page first |
| LinkedIn | 3 max | Industry terms only. No generic tags |
| X/Twitter | 1-2 | Only if trending or community-specific |

### Posting Cadence

- **X/Twitter:** 3-5x daily (threads 1-2x weekly)
- **LinkedIn:** 1x daily (carousels 2-3x weekly)
- **Instagram:** 1x daily feed + 3-5 Stories
- **TikTok:** 1-3x daily

### Engagement Rules

1. **Reply to every comment** in the first hour
2. **Ask a question** at the end of every post
3. **Tag people** only when genuinely relevant
4. **Cross-post** with platform-native formatting -- never copy-paste identical text

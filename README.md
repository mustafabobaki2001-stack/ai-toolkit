## Voiceover Scripts

Ready-to-record narration templates. Drop in your topic and facts, then send to ElevenLabs or your preferred TTS.

**Example -- The "Myth Buster" Short:**

```
Here's something most people get wrong about [TOPIC].

They think [COMMON MISCONCEPTION]. But the data tells a different story.

[SURPRISING FACT WITH SOURCE]. That changes everything -- because it means
[REAL-WORLD IMPLICATION].

The people who understand this? They're already [POSITIVE OUTCOME].
The rest are still stuck believing [MISCONCEPTION RESTATED].

Which side are you on?
```

## YouTube Hooks

The first 2 seconds decide whether someone stays or scrolls. These formulas are reverse-engineered from videos with 1M+ views.

**Example -- The "Insider Secret" Hook:**

```
Nobody is talking about this, and honestly, I understand why.
[TOPIC] just changed permanently -- and most people have no idea.
```

**Example -- The "Pattern Interrupt" Hook:**

```
Stop scrolling. I need 60 seconds.
What I'm about to show you about [TOPIC] took me [TIME PERIOD] to figure out.
```

**Example -- The "Counter-Intuitive" Hook:**

```
The worst advice I ever followed was "[COMMON ADVICE]."
Here's what actually works instead.
```

## Email Sequences

Complete sequence frameworks -- not single emails. Includes timing, subject lines, and body structure for each message in the sequence.

**Example -- Product Launch (5-email sequence):**

```
Email 1 (Day -3): "The Problem" -- Paint the pain point, no product mention
Email 2 (Day -1): "The Discovery" -- Share your journey finding the solution
Email 3 (Day 0):  "It's Live" -- Direct launch with social proof and urgency
Email 4 (Day +2): "The Results" -- Customer wins and testimonials
Email 5 (Day +5): "Last Chance" -- Scarcity close with bonus stack
```

## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Pick a template** that matches your content goal
2. **Replace `[PLACEHOLDERS]`** with your specific topic, data, and voice
3. **Feed to any LLM** (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) or use directly
4. **Customize the tone** -- these are frameworks, not scripts to read verbatim
5. **Test and iterate** -- track what resonates with your audience

### Tips for Best Results

- **Stack prompts**: Use a YouTube hook + voiceover script together for Shorts
- **A/B test hooks**: Try 3 different openers for the same content
- **Platform-adapt**: The same insight needs different framing for LinkedIn vs. TikTok
- **Add data**: Every template improves when you add a real number or stat
